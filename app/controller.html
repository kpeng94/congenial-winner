WASD or arrow keys for movement
QE for rotation
Spacebar to shoot
You gain 1 point to your personal score each time you shoot any other player.
You lose 1 point from your personal score each time you are shot (including by yourself).
Each player has two teammates.
These teammates are hidden from others and only shown on your individual screen.
Teams are asymmetric. This means that if Player 2 is on your team, you are not on Player 2’s team.
Your final score is the sum of your personal score and your teammates’ personal scores after 3 min.
The player with the highest final score wins.
The game lasts 3 min.
You have unlimited respawns.
You respawn automatically.
After respawn, you are invincible until you move/turn/shoot or until 10 seconds is up, whichevercomes first.
